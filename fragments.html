Welcome to Freckled Frosting

Freckled Frosting was founded by me, Renee Jackson. I started baking when I was about 12 years old and my dream for many years was to one day have my own bake shop where I could bring happiness to people through amazing sweet treats. My daughter, Kaylee, was my inspiration. I wanted to create a business that I could pass down to her. Kaylee being the most amazing person I know soon began calling me her “Cupcake Mommy”. I took this title very seriously and there within a name began my motivation for making my dreams come true.

We make a variety of different cupcakes but we do have gluten free and vegan varieties. The love for and from my family motivated me to start testing cupcakes specifically relating to vegan, gluten free and allergy free recipes. My husband, brother, aunts and cousins have many allergy and gluten restricted diets. This makes it almost impossible for them to eat anything that comes close to a great tasting, high quality baked good. It is my mission to help people overcome the problem of finding great tasting, high quality baked goods.
